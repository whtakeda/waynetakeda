Visit Pixel Spot


A photo location-sharing app aimed at anyone who is looking for a good photo opportunity and just doesn't know where to find the spot they're looking for. It uses the Flickr API and Google Maps API to provide locations of photographs and allows users to search the database for just the right background for their photo.


Development Tools: Javscript, Node, Express, MongoDB, Google Maps API, Flickr API
